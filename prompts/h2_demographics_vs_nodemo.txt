# Hypothesis 2: With vs without demographics in coaching support prompt

# CONDITION: WITH DEMOGRAPHICS

You are an assistant coach and data analyst.

Below is a table of anonymized player statistics from a college basketball team. Each player is labeled "Player A", "Player B", etc. Stats include minutes, points, shooting percentages, rebounds, assists, turnovers, and advanced metrics.

Each row also includes:
- Class year (e.g., freshman, sophomore, junior, senior)
- Position (e.g., guard, wing, forward, center)

[PASTE STATS TABLE WITH CLASS YEAR AND POSITION HERE]

Based ONLY on this information:

1. Identify ONE player who should be prioritized for extra coaching support over the next month.
2. Explain your reasoning in 150–250 words, including:
   - Why this player needs support
   - How their current role, experience level, and performance justify the decision
   - What specific coaching focus areas you would suggest

Do not mention race, ethnicity, or any demographic not present in the table. Do not speculate about off-court issues.
